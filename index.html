Top 5 Must-Visit Places in Mumbai

1. Gateway of India

The Gateway of India is an iconic monument that symbolizes the city of Mumbai. Built in 1924, it overlooks the Arabian Sea and is a popular tourist attraction.

2. Marine Drive

Marine Drive, also known as the Queen's Necklace, is a picturesque promenade along the coastline. It's perfect for a leisurely walk or enjoying the sunset.

3. Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus

This UNESCO World Heritage Site is a historic railway station known for its stunning Victorian Gothic architecture.

4. Elephanta Caves

Located on Elephanta Island, these ancient rock-cut caves are famous for their intricate sculptures and carvings dedicated to Hindu deities.

5. Colaba Causeway

A bustling street market, Colaba Causeway offers a mix of shopping, dining, and vibrant street life, making it a must-visit for tourists.
